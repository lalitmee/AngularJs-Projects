Manmeet Singh

meethaz@outlook.com

Manmeet has over 21 years of professional experience as Application Developer, Tech Team Leader, Delivery Manager, Account Manager, Delivery Project Executive, VP-Delivery, Entrepreneur, Program manager of a large social initiative and a budding Life/Professional Coach.

Manmeet gets a ‘high’ when he is able to convert a ‘Vision’ to a working plan and energise his team to achieve the ‘Vision’. Manmeet considers himself to be good at communicating the larger picture and connecting the entire team to it.

In the last 2 years Manmeet has spent considerable time building his startup Samvad – www.lestsamvad.com from a idea to MVP, running through the pilot phase and currently being Incubated at NSRCEL at IIMB.

Manmeet is currently uniquely placed with his recent entrepreneurial journey of Samvad, working as a program manager for a large social initiative in the education sector and completing his coach training program from Coach for Life, USA, and working towards to shortly be certified as a Associate Certified Coach by ICF. Manmeet has been fortunate to be trained by the Master Coach, Peter Reding of Coach for Life.

Manmeet is well equipped to coach high performing IT professionals in India in their mid-career looking to reenergize and reinvent themselves. This deep belief comes from Manmeet’s professional journey experiences and his recent commitment to be a ICF certified coach in 2018.

Manmeet is a Computer Science Engineer by education, a keen observer and people professional by choice. Manmeet completed his Advanced Management Program from IIMB in 2014
